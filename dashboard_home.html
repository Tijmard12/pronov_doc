Sale

The following are to be noted in a transaction.

  • Enter the Product Name or the Nafdac Number (e.g Paracetamol Inj 15ml)
  • A page will prompt out requesting for the quantity and quantity type(either blister pack or carton) of the product to be sold, and tap "SAVE" to continue
  • A page showing the transaction details will appear and tap "SELL" to proceed
  • A page will prompt out requesting for the customer's detail i.e Contact number, Name, Email Address(if any), brief note concerning the product to be sold if neccesary, and the payment option ( either by CASH or MPOS). Tap "SAVE" to save or "CANCEL" to cancel

Query Transaction

This following are to be noted do incur a transaction.

  • Enter the date and time of the range you want to fetch the transaction details.
  • Tap on "QUERY TRANSACTION" to proceed.
  • The transactions details will appear from the range you entered.
  • Each detail can be accessed by tapping on them.
